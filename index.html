Consistent with the philosophy of the school, the pursuit of excellence underlines the philosophy of the Library Information Centre. The Library exists to facilitate and to assist the investigation of human knowledge and the acquisition of research skills for both teachers and students. It is our belief that the future welfare of our students is as good as the quality of information available to them. In modern times this translates into the provision of the best available resources in both print and non-print format including online databases, e-books and the Internet.
